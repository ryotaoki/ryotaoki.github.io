FAQ

We provide tutoring mainly for elementary, middle, and high school subjects including English, French, German, Spanish,and Japanese. We also offer support for university entrance exams and language proficiency tests.

Lessons are conducted online via Zoom or Google Meet. After each session, we provide a summary report including lesson details and assignments.

Our standard rate is 2,500 JPY per 60-minute session. We also offer discounted packages for multiple sessions booked in advance.

You can book lessons through our online reservation form by selecting your preferred date, time, and subject. Our tutors will confirm your booking within 24 hours.

All tutors have at least a university degree and possess teaching experience or relevant certifications. We conduct mock lessons and interviews to ensure high teaching quality and communication skills.

Cancellations or rescheduling requests made at least 24 hours before the lesson are free of charge. Cancellations within 24 hours of the lesson will incur a 50% fee.
